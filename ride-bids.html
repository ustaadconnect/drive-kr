How Bidding Works
  1. Drivers in your area see your ride request
  2. They bid below your maximum price (Rs 500)
  3. You can accept any bid, wait for more, or send counter offers
  4. Once you accept a bid, driver details will be shared via WhatsApp
  5. 15% commission will be deducted from the final price
  6. You can rate the driver after ride completion
